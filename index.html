About Me

My name is Jianleeu S. Quiñones Cruz, I am a student in the graphic design program at Inter San Germán. During my development in graphic design, I have found myself working more towards a minimalist style. By working with a minimalist design, it allows you to represent what you want, eliminating noise, unnecessary elements and overload. It is a style that allows you to present what is important and fundamental in the design without straying from its identity. The mission of seeking simplicity and order in a chaotic world is what attracts me to minimalism.
